My Strengths and Weaknesses

On this page, you will see what my strengths and weaknesses are.

My Strengths

Leadership Skills

I have the instinct to step up
and take the lead in situations
that requires a leader.

Dedicated

I am dedicated when I am tasked
to do something and I give my
best in all my works.

Critical Thinking

I am quick in decision-making and
I always keep an open mind with
the environment I am in.

Teamwork

I am able to work at any environment
and with people I just met.

Communication Skills

I am able to communicate with
my team and even with new people
in my environment.

Creativity

I always try to give my best with
things I do and present the best I
have in showing my creative
knowledge and skills.

Honest

I am very honest with my work and
my responsibilities. I always show
honesty when I'm working
with people.

Empathetic

I show empathy to all people and
I always try to understand situations
in the best way possible.

Responsible

I am very responsible when tasks are
given to me and I always finish
them before the given time.

Positivity

I always keep a positive mindset when
I am working and especially in a
hectic and stressed enviroment.


My Weaknesses

Multitasking

Sometimes, I disregard my work
when the deadline is long that
results to my work piling up.

Unable to say "no"

Because of my instinct in leading,
when people needs me, I always
have a hard time saying no.

Insecure

Most of the time, I am insecure
with my work, thinking its not
good enough.

Self-crticism

I always criticize my work,
my skills, and myself, doubting
if I am able to do a specific task.

Unable to ask for help

I don't want to be a bother to
anyone so asking for help is
hard for me.
